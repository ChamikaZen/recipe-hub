Dessert Recipes

Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients:

  • Dark chocolate
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Flour

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Melt chocolate and butter together.
  3. Whisk in eggs and sugar, then flour.
  4. Pour into ramekins and bake for 12 minutes.

Strawberry Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • Cream cheese
  • Graham crackers
  • Sugar
  • Strawberries
  • Whipped cream

Steps:

  1. Prepare crust with crushed graham crackers and butter.
  2. Mix cream cheese and sugar until smooth.
  3. Pour into crust and chill for 4 hours.
  4. Top with strawberries and whipped cream before serving.
