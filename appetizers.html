Appetizer Recipes

Bruschetta

Ingredients:

  • Baguette
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Basil
  • Olive oil

Steps:

  1. Toast the baguette slices.
  2. Mix chopped tomatoes, garlic, and basil.
  3. Top bread with the mixture and drizzle with olive oil.

Stuffed Mushrooms

Ingredients:

  • Mushrooms
  • Cream cheese
  • Parmesan
  • Garlic
  • Bread crumbs

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Remove stems from mushrooms and mix filling.
  3. Stuff mushrooms and bake for 20 minutes.
